Christopher Benjamin, PhD is a clinical neuropsychologist in Vancouver who provides comprehensive assessments of memory, attention, and thinking. His evaluations help clarify diagnoses and guide treatment, education, and life planning.

With training at UCLA, Harvard, and the University of Melbourne, and appointments at Vancouver General Hospital and Yale University, Dr. Benjamin brings a warm, collaborative, and science-grounded approach to every assessment.
