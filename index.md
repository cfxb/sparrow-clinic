Christopher Benjamin, PhD is a clinical neuropsychologist in Vancouver who assesses memory, attention, and thinking in adults aged 16 and older. His evaluations help clarify diagnoses and guide treatment, education, and life planning.

With training at UCLA, Harvard, and the University of Melbourne, and appointments at Vancouver General Hospital and Yale University, Dr. Benjamin combines standardised testing with evidence-based interpretation for every assessment.
